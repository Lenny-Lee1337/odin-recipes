Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian dish

This pasta dish is easy to make, and everyone loves it

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs of choice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil pasta in water until desired consistency, then strain
  2. Saute onions and garlic until softened
  3. Add ground beef, saute until browned
  4. Add Tomato sauce, salt, pepper to taste and simmer until thickened
  5. Serve pasta on a dish and top with meat sauce
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs
